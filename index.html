How to Play

Dash around the glowing rings, dodge the cyan hazards, and collect power-ups.

  • Move: ⬅️ ➡️ or A / D
  • Hop to next ring: SPACE
  • Gold orbs trigger bullet-time, then a burst of speed.
  • Blue orbs slow you down.
  • Purple orbs grant 5 seconds of invincibility.
  • Survive as long as you can across the five orbits.
